As Deadd doesn't work correctly at least on my convertible and brings problems
on desktop too, I decided to try something else. And found [[https://github.com/ErikReider/SwayNotificationCenter][Sway Notification
Center]].

So far it is working directly from Guix, so I just added
="swaynotificationcenter"= to list of packages.
